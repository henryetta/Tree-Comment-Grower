Privacy Policy - Tree Growth Extension

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction

This Chrome extension ("Tree Growth Extension") is part of an academic research study investigating gamification approaches to encourage positive social media behavior. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data We Collect

The extension collects the following information:

  • Email Address: Used solely for lottery winner notifications
  • Social Media Comments: Comments you post on supported platforms (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit) are analyzed to determine sentiment
  • Tree Growth Data: Your tree planting choices, growth progress, and completion status
  • Weekly Statistics: Aggregated data on your commenting activity and sentiment scores
  • Leaderboard Data: Your rank and points for monthly competitions
  • Extension Usage: Basic analytics on feature usage and engagement

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Sentiment Analysis: Comments are analyzed using AI to categorize them as positive, neutral, or negative. Positive comments contribute to tree growth
  • Gamification: Data is used to calculate tree growth, award lottery tickets, and maintain leaderboards
  • Notifications: Email is used only to notify lottery winners at the end of each month (top 50 users receive lottery tickets to spin a reward wheel)
  • Research: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used in academic publications about social media behavior and gamification

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in Supabase (a secure cloud database platform)
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only the research team has access to identifiable data
  • Your data is NOT sold or shared with third parties
  • Your data is NOT shared with social media platforms

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for the duration of the research study (approximately 12 weeks from your first use) plus an additional period for analysis. After the study concludes, identifiable information may be anonymized or deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all data we have collected about you
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw: Stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it
  • Opt Out: Decline participation in the research study while still using the extension

7. Research Study Information

  • Study Title: Gamifying Positive Social Media Behavior Through Tree Growth Visualization
  • Institution: Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology, South Korea
  • Study Duration: 8-12 weeks per participant

8. Third-Party Services

This extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and backend functionality
  • Built in Custom AI Sentiment Analysis: Your comments are analyzed using a personalized built-in AI model to determine sentiment (positive, neutral, or negative)

Comment text is processed by our AI system solely for sentiment analysis purposes. The AI model is part of the research infrastructure and does not share data with external AI service providers.

9. Permissions Justification

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • Storage: To save your user ID and extension settings locally
  • Host Permissions: To detect when you post comments on supported social media platforms
  • Scripting: To integrate with social media pages and detect comment submissions

10. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are under 18, please do not use this extension.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension interface. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
